🎬 StreamProbe YouTube Testing

Testing Instructions:

  1. Load the StreamProbe extension in Chrome
  2. Click one of the YouTube test links below
  3. Play the video on YouTube
  4. Click the StreamProbe extension icon
  5. Click "Start Monitoring" in the popup
  6. Observe speed indicators (Fast/Good/Okay/Slow)
  7. Test different network conditions (3G/4G/5G)
  8. Check buffer health and latency status
⚠️ Important: YouTube detection may take a few seconds as the extension waits for the video player to fully load. You may see "No video detected" initially - this is normal!

Test YouTube Videos:

🎵 Music Video (Short) 🎥 Demo Video (Long) 🔬 Tech Talk 📺 HD Content

What to Look For:

Speed Indicators:

  • 🟢 FAST - Buffer ahead ≥10s (Excellent)
  • 🟢 GOOD - Buffer ahead 5-10s (Good)
  • 🟡 OKAY - Buffer ahead 2-5s (Fair)
  • 🔴 SLOW - Buffer ahead <2s (Poor)

Latency Status:

  • 🚀 Excellent (<50ms)
  • ✅ Good (50-100ms)
  • ⚠️ Fair (100-200ms)
  • 🐌 Poor (>200ms)
💡 Tip: If the extension doesn't detect the video immediately, try refreshing the YouTube page and starting monitoring again. YouTube's dynamic loading can sometimes delay video element detection.

Troubleshooting:

  • "No video detected" - Wait a few seconds, YouTube is still loading
  • Extension not working - Check console logs (F12) for error messages
  • Speed always shows "Slow" - Video might be paused or buffering
  • Network throttling not working - Ensure debugger permission is granted
